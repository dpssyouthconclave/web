DiPS Film Festival

Edutainment through the lens

Filmmaking is a very complex process which takes a lifetime to learn but is a very powerful medium of edutainment. Keeping in mind the rising number of students interested in filmmaking, Delhi Public School, Bangalore south takes an initiative to tap young enthusiasm to meditate on the art and craft of this beautiful medium. A two days film festival dated November 2 and 3, 2018 has been arranged for students of Classes 7-12, to encourage aspiring filmmakers to develop their own worldview and initiate into the magical world of cinema.

Competitive level

  • CATEGORY A

  • CATEGORY B

  • CATEGORY C

Entries by students of classes 11 & 12

General conditions and guidelines for applicants

a.The short films are to be submitted by 30th October, 2018. Students are to submit it online on the link in the website. They may also send a pen drive or CD to the school. Mail address as follows: Delhi Public School Bangalore South
11 K M, Kanakapura Main Road, Bangalore - 560062

b.Entry fee: Rs.500/- per entry per category
c.The school can submit any number of entries in the categories mentioned. Separate entry form is required for each entry.
d.The short film must be substantially in English. A few phrases in other languages are accepted.
e.Captions and credits must be clear, legible and must be within the safe areas.
f.Organiser/s
  • will have the right to screen the awarded / nominated films as and when required for academic and non-commercial screening
  • reserve the right to reject any entry if found ineligible
g.Pen-drive of films submitted for the competition will be retained by the organisers.
h.It is essential to fill all the columns in the entry form, failing which the entry is liable to be rejected.
i.The film should not violate the copyright / intellectual property law. In case such a violation is reported at a later date the awardee/s will have to return the cash prize, trophy and certificate.

How to participate

  1. Please read carefully terms & conditions of the film festival before filling the form.
  2. Fill one entry form for each film.
  3. The script of the film is to be submitted online by 25th October, 2018.
  4. Pen drive if submitted, should contain the following :
    • Copy of the film in mp4 format.
    • Synopsis of the film in 200 – 250 words (in MS Word doc.file)
    • Five still photographs of the film (jpeg format) and one photograph of the director (jpeg format) is to be submitted on 30th October, 2018.
  5. Letter of acknowledgement from the Head of the institution (must be on official letter head with official seal) is to be submitted on the day of the competition.
  6. A hard copy of the completely filled Entry Form is also to be submitted during registration.

Selection for the festival

  1. The decision of Jury will be final.
  2. The films will be showcased in specific time slots on the days of the festival.
  3. 5 representatives from the crew of each shortlisted film will be invited to attend the festival.
  4. Organisers reserve the right to modify the rules and conditions including number and amount of awards.

NOTE

  • Please ensure that the short film is saved in the right format as specified, on a pen-drive/ CD.
