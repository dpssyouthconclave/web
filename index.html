AHAM

AHAM - the first ever ‘Youth Conclave’ to be organized by a school in Bangalore city will bring about a confluence of students from schools across Bangalore. The 2 day event scheduled for November 2 and 3, 2018 will engage students in thought provoking sessions designed to enhance individual and collective capacities.


We believe that the youth are front runners in nation building. The endeavour is to facilitate the building of self – concept and self – image. Creativity is the fountainhead of most marvels and we seek to promote ‘uniqueness’ in every child. We believe that every child is talented and ample opportunities are provided to nourish latent talent and potential.

DiPS Film Festival

Edutainment through the lens

Filmmaking is a very complex process which takes a lifetime to learn but is a very powerful medium of edutainment. Keeping in mind the rising number of students interested in filmmaking, Delhi Public School, Bangalore south takes an initiative to tap young enthusiasm to meditate on the art and craft of this beautiful medium.

By Dr.Sathyaki Nambala Senior Consultant, Cardio-Thoracic and Vascular Surgery

Dr Sathyaki Nambala is one of the leading authorities in the Country in the treatment of heart, lung and vascular surgery. He is the Asia’s first Totally Endoscopic Cardiac Keyhole surgeon.
Dr. Sathyaki has vast experience in complex valve repairs and is well known for his skills in complicated cardiac operations.Fellow at the Toronto General Hospital’s The Peter Munk Cardiac Center in Canada.Senior Fellow in Cardiac surgery at the Harvard Medical School.He has gained recognition for performing complicated heart valve replacements/repairs via a keyhole also termed Totally Endoscopic Heart Surgery.He also has significant expertise in pediatric cardiac surgery.
Dr. Nambala’s expertise among others include Aortic aneurysm surgery, Aortic valve repair, complex aortic root surgery, minimally invasive valve and CABG surgery, Heart failure surgery including artificial heart (Assist Devices) and heart transplant to name a few. He is experienced in redo surgery as well.

Keynote Session

By SANJAY ANANDARAM

Sanjay has spent over two decades as an IT industry executive, an entrepreneur, a start-up mentor, and a VC and investment advisor. He brings significant experience in creating new businesses and in business development in India, USA, Asia, Middle-East, and Africa. Earlier, Sanjay was a founding partner at JumpStartUp an early stage VC fund set up in 2000 that invested in early-stage technology and technology enabled businesses in India and the US. Prior to JumpStartUp, Sanjay founded Neta Inc., acquired by Infoseek (Disney), and VentureKatalyst, India's first online publication aimed at entrepreneurs. Before Neta, Sanjay was at Wipro in Global R&D, where he headed several new business development initiatives in India and USA.
Sanjay is a Charter Member at TiE, Bangalore, where he co-chairs the Entrepreneur Acceleration Programme. He is also involved with Nasscom's Product and Innovation group, and IIM Bangalore's NS Raghavan Centre for Entrepreneurial Learning. Sanjay writes and lectures frequently on entrepreneurship, innovation and venture capital. has been Adjunct Faculty at At INSEAD, Singapore, and on the Expert Panel of the Technology Development Board at Government of India's Department of Science and Technology, Sanjay is an Electrical Engineer from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, with an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore.

Keynote Session

By Nirupama and Rajendra

Nirupama & Rajendra are one of India's foremost classical dancers, who hail from a family of educationists, artists and Harikatha Vidwans (Traditional story tellers of Karnataka). As founders & artistic directors of ‘Abhinava Dance Company’, the duo has introduced a great deal of innovation, creativity, enthusiasm and imagination to Classical Art. They have been incessantly working in the field of dance for 25 years as dancers, choreographers, teachers, producers and performers in their own right. Their performances are based on Indian classical dance perfected over the years from their learning in music, dance, literature and culture from various sources (some that date back to 5000 year old scriptures to 21st century technological innovations).

Keynote Session

By Syed Sultan Ahmed

Syed Sultan Ahmed, the founder of LXL IDEAS, has been an advisor to CIET-NCERT and is a member of the FICCI School Education Committee. He also serves as an advisor to several children’s brands and educational institutions globally on issues related to the psychosocial development of teenagers.
LXL Ideas which also runs a film-based module ‘School Cinema’ where they produce films for children. The intent of making films is to teach children important life skills, which in the longer run prove beneficial.
LXL Ideas translates to ‘learning the x factor of life’. Syed says it is about emphasis on life skills’ education.
The movies advocates values like honesty, respecting elders, following traffic rules and touches upon topics like adolescence issues, peer issues, suicides, and religious and cultural ssensitivity. The efforts have made off. In six years, they bagged six national awards, which Syed feels, is a testimony to the quality of films they make for children. Over one million children are using the project to learn life lessons as part of their curriculum through 120 plus films.
